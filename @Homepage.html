Our Environment


Enviroment is the surrounding in which we live. But the contamination of our environment by pollutants is environmental pollution. The current stage of the earth that we are seeing is the cause of centuries of exploitation of the earth and its resources.

It is very important to take care of our environment because our health and survival on the Earth directly depend on the health of our environment. It is very easy to find dirt and pollution in many places across the world and a large population of the world is living in these bad and unhygienic conditions.
